CMS Solutions

“Content Management System + Customer Relationship Management will grow your business.”

Choosing the right Content Management System (CMS) is crucial need for all business houses. Ranging from blogs to an e-commerce application, a CMS is capable of delivering the most effective solution of adding, updating and deleting fundamental parts of your web product. Kolaza helps Relations round the globe scale their business using the best suited CMS.

CMS applications comes in all flavours, from open source to paid licenses there is an ocean of software on the internet. It is only by careful analyses of the business needs that a perfect solution could be picked up.

Every business has its own need and thus a single CMS does not fit in all verticals. Kolaza solves the puzzle to choose the best CMS solution best suited to your business needs. We experience in number of available applications.

Analysing the Business needs of our Relations.

Suggesting different available free and open source CMS / CRM, further discussing pros and cons of each

Picking the appropriate design for the UI.

Implementing the CMS / CRM solution on a secure cloud platform.

Optimizing the solution to comply with best web security standards.

Delivering the solution on time following best practices.

Life time maintenance of the solution.

A CMS has be further broken into content management application (CMA) and content delivery application (CDA). CMA builds backend while the CDA controls the user interface. CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and CMS (Content Management System) are two different categorization of the applications, but with growing demands of users nearly all CRM solutions can be integrated with most CMS efficiently.

Generating leads and keeping customers happy is quite steady and an uphill task in the current competitive world. Leads are first point of sales and hence a business houses always keep their sleeves rolled when it comes to implementing a CRM software. Studying and iterating over majorly existing CRM solutions we have mastered ourselves to tailor the product for you.
