Startup Prototyping

“Respect the X factor of Entrepreneurs”

Finding the right partner for your big idea is the biggest challenge till date for most of the entrepreneurs. Great partnerships with brilliant ideas and excellent teams fail to convince the product to investor on the demo-day. A dedicated team at Kolaza, solves this problem. We help you with alternatives to attract users or investors.

We build working, demo-day prototypes for your big ideas. Carefully examining the idea and then quickly building the product over it. With the best technologies under our belt, we turn imaginations into working web pages. Using cutting edge, high performance and scalable web technologies we satisfy the hunger of entrepreneurs.
